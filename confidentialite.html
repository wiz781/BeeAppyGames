Cube Match Odyssey - Privacy Policy

Beeappy respects your privacy.

We only collect the information necessary for the game to function properly, such as your Google ID, username, and email address if you log in via Google.

This information is used solely to synchronize your game data and is never sold.

Data Deletion Request – Cube Match Odyssey

The application Cube Match Odyssey allows users to request the deletion of their personal data in accordance with applicable data protection regulations (GDPR, Google Play policies).

How to request data deletion

To request the deletion of your data, please send an email to: beeappygames@gmail.com

In your message, please include:

- The name of the application: Cube Match Odyssey

- Your player identifier (if known)

- A clear request for data deletion

Data that will be deleted

After your request is processed, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

- User identifier (Unity Authentication)

- Game progress

- Player nickname and avatar

- Leaderboard data

- Cloud save data (Unity Cloud Save)

Processing time

Data deletion requests are processed within a maximum period of 30 days.
